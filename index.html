Engineering insights
Drag reduction
Cd < 0.05 is excellent for streamlined bodies. Cd > 0.3 indicates separation. BL trips can reduce pressure drag 15–30%.
Thermal management
Nu > 200 at moderate Re suggests turbulent transition. Stagnation zones concentrate heat. Add fins where q″ > 2000 W/m².
L/D optimization
L/D > 15 targets efficient lifting surfaces. Optimal AoA for max L/D is typically 3°–6° for NACA profiles.
